There are many ways to learn about cancer. One can search Google, watch Youtube videos, or peruse articles off news sites. The Cancer Feed aggregates all of those mediums into one place. It is meant for anyone who is interested in learning about cancer.


Here's what the Cancer Feed provides:





News

Using real-time feed technology, the News page offers the latest articles from established institutions, including Cancer Research UK, Medical News Today, and the National Institute of Cancer. The page is updated every time a new article is published

Resources

The Resources page includes a list of educational videos ranging various topics including cancer and research. Each video is selected from the Yale-NUS Canvas site for the Cancer unit of Foundations of Science.





Learning

Feeling adventurous? Learn will take you to a random page that has information about some aspect of cancer. It makes sure the websites are credible, only connecting to those of established institutions such as the National Institute of Cancer, Cancer.gov, American Cancer Society, and Cancer Research UK. The topics includes a wide variety such as cancer therapies, prevention, risk factors, and general statistics.

Feedback

Once you are done exploring the site, please give feedback! Your suggestions and comments will greatly help the site improve.
